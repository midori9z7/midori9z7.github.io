Midori - Transform (2026)

Transform 

We degenerate into adults and evolve into puppies
The sweetness of death shows the silence you adore.
Beneath the snow, rivers flow into each other, charging into each other
While the snow is deep on the ground.
The firewood ignited by an angel
Always the smoke and light fall
Heavily down on your stretched back.

When the tide is asleep, I'd say: let us sink together.
Softly, turn around, put down any thoughts for the time being
Ignore the withered waving hands of the sea
And make the sky know of our love.

Hold up the skirt of our goddess with solid eyes,
Fulfill every hair with imaginary clouds
Rising suns, rising woods we couldn't go through
You shall not forget me
Who made me think of the collapse of the stone pillar
And the two poles of the earth.
From tomorrow, look at the purple-green agate arc.
Theoretically, this is the end we should get...

God's feathers surround me and caress my hair.
Kiss my lips and make me cry. 
I was finding your angelic blood stains
Though it captured my soul.

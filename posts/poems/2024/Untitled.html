Midori - Untitled (2024)

Untitled 

Vapor weeps on the thousand-lake plateau, each tile a tongue.
Dusk is dissolving into a glass.
Wash the cross in the mirror, back and forth,
until your lashes frost with rime and spume—
the hostage whisper of a coward's breath.
A loosened watch is wedged inside the collar, ticking against skin.
Morning drapes a golden wedding dress over the yak's broad back.
All earth's marriages are bartered only by nature's hand.
Let the bathtub bear the birthmark of twin moons.

Again your fingerprints bleed onto the window's rim.
The words hurriedly fell on the glue of the distant moon.
wait till the whole city's reflection oozes salt grains.
Let's go and buy a watch woven from deep nets.

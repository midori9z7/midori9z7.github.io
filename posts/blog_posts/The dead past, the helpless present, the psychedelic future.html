The dead past, the helpless present, the psychedelic future

So after graduating from junior high school, I finally have some spare time to write a blog from scratch and update posts at the same time. I once wrote WeChat official account before, but I didn't stick to it. Now I blog, and you can treat it as a grave. The words I written down are the epitaph. Long live the DIGITAL LIFE!!! It's a pity that I probably won't live the day when digital life really comes true.
Actually I read a sentence before that if you consciously lose more and achieve less, and have no ambition, is it possible that the world has not reserved anything that can be achieved and can be achieved for you at all?
I wish I could go back in time, back to the summer that was said million times, the carefree summer when I was a child. It was extremely cool in my mind. Is there really so much difference between the ignorant and the fearless in reality?
When I return to the age of sixteen, can being loved actually make people happy? Or it's a tragedy, a floating illusion, a paralyzing psychology, a desire that makes you addicted, a fantasy that makes you always think that this rotten world still leaves you a corner to be reckless. Does it? I don't think so. I think being loved is a fortunate thing. I love more when I can't get it. Pathetically I've only experienced it a few times, and I fucked up every time. Then it is not something worth looking forward to at all. I can only feel the truth of the world when I suppress my crying in despair. But I am extremely reluctant to do this.
But what else can I do? Im fucking poor and ugly. This kind of ugliness has even pierced my heart and made me inferior and suspicious. I have to push away any happiness, and I have to cast contempt for any beauty. When I look back again, that big tree that allowed me to rest and enjoy has been burned down, and my future is a plateau surrounded by wind, thunder and fog, and all of this is caused by me. I want to build a vehicle to cross it, but there was almost nothing left for me in the past, and I didn't even fucking expect it since a long time ago .
I always feel that I'm living like a weed. It's the early morning of July 12th. The typhoon has just arrived. Let the wind blow the weed in wherever the direction it wants.
Nevertheless, I still have someone I care about. I think it's better to continue to hide the emotions. After all, time has passed, and my relationship with her has long been gone.
But I will always remember her until death separates my body and soul.
I don't want to say that I can't live without her. It's unrealistic. In fact, I don't need a close relationship to live, and no one needs me to create this kind of relationship. I can confess it here just because she won't visit my blog. It's late at night. I think that's all for this article. I'm sad.
I saw a comment for a game on Steam before, and I want to take this sentence as the end of my first blog post.
Is it wrong to live in the past?
Maybe it's really wrong.

Thanks for reading my blog post.
